## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit our website** at greensiliconvalley.org
2. **Click "Sign Up"** or "Get Involved"
3. **Choose your role** (Teacher, Volunteer, or Intern)
4. **Complete your profile** with relevant information
5. **Wait for approval** (if required for your role)

### Account Approval Process

| Your Role | Approval Time | What Happens Next |
|-----------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Teacher** | Instant | Access dashboard immediately |
| **Volunteer** | 1-3 business days | Email notification when approved |
| **Intern** | 1-3 business days | Email notification when approved |

### First Login Experience

When you first log in, you'll be guided through:

1. **Role confirmation** (if needed)
2. **Profile completion**
3. **Onboarding tutorial**
4. **Dashboard setup**

**Dynamic Forms:**
Our platform uses smart forms that adapt based on your answers:

1. **Required fields** are marked with red asterisks (*)
2. **Conditional fields** appear based on previous answers
3. **File uploads** support common formats (PDF, DOC, JPG, PNG)
4. **Validation** helps ensure complete and accurate submissions

## Permissions & Access

### Understanding Permissions

**What are permissions?**
Permissions control what you can see and do on the platform. They ensure everyone has appropriate access while protecting sensitive information.

**Types of Permissions:**
- **View permissions**: See specific content or data
- **Edit permissions**: Modify content or settings
- **Create permissions**: Add new content or resources
- **Delete permissions**: Remove content or data
- **Admin permissions**: Manage users and system settings

### Requesting Additional Permissions

**When to request permissions:**
- Need access to specific resources for your work
- Want to contribute content in restricted areas
- Require editing access for assigned projects

**How to request:**
1. Go to your dashboard
2. Find "Request Permissions" section
3. Select permission type and resource
4. Explain why you need access
5. Submit request for approval

**Approval Process:**
- Requests reviewed within 1-2 business days
- May include discussion with your supervisor
- Approved permissions may have expiration dates
- Can be revoked if no longer needed

### Permission Levels

| Permission Level | What You Can Do | Examples |
|------------------|-----------------|----------|
| **Basic** | View public content, submit forms | All registered users |
| **Standard** | Edit own content, access member resources | Approved volunteers/teachers |
| **Advanced** | Create content, manage resources | Interns with specific roles |
| **Admin** | Full system access, user management | Founders and administrators |

### Troubleshooting

**Common Issues:**

**Can't access a feature?**
- Check your account permissions
- Request additional access if needed
- Contact support if you believe it's an error

**Form not submitting?**
- Check for required fields (marked with *)
- Ensure all information is complete
- Try refreshing the page
- Contact support if issues persist

**Content not loading?**
- Clear your browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Check your internet connection
- Contact technical support

**Login problems?**
- Reset your password if forgotten
- Check that you're using the correct email
- Clear browser cookies and cache
- Contact support for account issues
